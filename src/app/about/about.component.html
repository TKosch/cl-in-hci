You can suggest new papers by filling out our Google Form. This supports us and the community to maintain an up-to-date version of the paper library. We require all indexed publications be peer reviewed, in English, archival, and relevant to cognitive workload in Human-Computer Interaction. The authors of the paper manage the paper library.

Corrections or additions to included publications are also welcome. We are very open to making edits or adding labels we may have missed.
