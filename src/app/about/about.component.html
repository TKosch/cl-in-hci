This webpage runs a paper library about relevant papers that investigated the semantics and measurements of cognitive workload in Human-Computer Interaction. This library was originally developed for a literature review that is based on this paper:

T. Kosch, J. Karolus, J. Zagermann, H. Reiterer, A. Schmidt, and P. Wozniak, ``A survey on measuring cognitive workload in human-computer interaction'', ACM Computing Surveys, 2023.

Open Access Paper (PDF)

You can suggest new papers by clicking on "Add an Article" and filling out the Google Form. This supports us and the community to maintain an up-to-date version of the paper library. We require all indexed publications to be peer reviewed, in English language, archival, and relevant to cognitive workload in Human-Computer Interaction. The authors of the paper manage the paper library.

Corrections or additions to included publications are also welcome. We are open to making edits or adding labels we may have missed.
